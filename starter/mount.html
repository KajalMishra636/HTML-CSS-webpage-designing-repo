What makes mountains special?

Mountains are formed by geological processes that create stunning landscapes full of diversity. From snow-capped peaks to lush valleys, each mountain range tells a story of nature's power and beauty.

They are home to unique flora and fauna, offer incredible trekking experiences, and often serve as cultural and spiritual symbols.

When exploring mountains, you'll encounter:

  1. Breathtaking views
  2. Challenging trails
  3. Rich biodiversity
