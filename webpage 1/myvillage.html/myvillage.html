Agriculture

Bihar is known for its fertile land. Villagers grow rice, wheat, maize, and sugarcane with traditional farming methods.

Culture & Festivals

Chhath Puja, Holi, and Diwali are celebrated with devotion. Folk songs and dances add color to village life.

Historic Places

Places like Bodh Gaya and Nalanda reflect Bihar's deep spiritual and educational history.

Nature & Rivers

Rivers like the Ganga and Koshi pass through villages, providing water, beauty, and livelihood.
